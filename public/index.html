Billing is monthly through Stripe. A completed audit is a successful scan that returns a report. Unused audits do not roll over in the current app.

When the monthly audit limit is reached, the app blocks additional audits until usage is available again in the billing period.

FAQ

Questions about the website audit platform.

Search the most common questions agencies ask before creating an account.

What does the platform scan?

Website health, SEO visibility, trust, conversion, technical health, broken pages, performance, and visual trust signals.

Can I white-label the app?

Yes. Agency branding can apply to the dashboard, scanner, report pages, PDF exports, favicon, browser title, and contact information.

Can I download reports as PDFs?

Yes. Saved reports can be downloaded as branded PDF reports from inside the authenticated app.

Are reports saved?

Yes. Reports are saved to the authenticated user's workspace and can be reopened later.

How many audits are included?

The Professional plan includes 100 completed audits per billing period.

Can prospects see your branding?

The public website uses W Strategies branding. The app and reports can be presented under the agency brand.

Does the platform build websites?

No. It creates professional website assessments that agencies can use in sales conversations and proposals.

Does it guarantee closed clients?

No. The platform does not guarantee sales results. It helps agencies present clearer evidence and recommendations.
