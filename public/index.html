How It Works

A premium walkthrough from account to sales call.

The workflow is designed to help agencies move quickly from prospect research to a clear website conversation.

01Create account
02Add branding
03Run scan
04Present report

Billing details

Billing is monthly through Stripe. A completed assessment is a successful scan that returns a report.

Rollover balance

Unused scans roll into the next billing period up to one additional month of allowance.

Plan changes

Plans can be managed through the secure Stripe billing portal inside the application.

What counts as a completed assessment?

A successful scan that returns a report counts toward the monthly allowance. Failed scans and public demo reports do not count.

Can I cancel through Stripe?

Subscription management is handled through the Stripe billing portal inside the authenticated app.

Are PDFs included?

Yes. Starter PDFs include Website Strategy Scan branding. Professional and Growth PDFs can be fully white-labeled with your agency branding.

The platform uses account authentication, saved reports, agency branding information, billing status, and website scan inputs to operate the service. Reports belong to authenticated users and are protected by user-specific access rules.
